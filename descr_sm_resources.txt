;    TRADE  RESOURCES

; Reviewed, slightly remoded and supplemented with explanations for SSHIP 097 (Summer 2020).
; See also descr_strat.txt - many provinces have precise explanations why a particular resource is there.
;
; Note: grain does not increase the growth of population.
;
; Ideas not used:
; - ceramics - already covered by the concept of glass; it was also produced rather locally.
; - sulphur (and other minerals - but non-metal) - used in ceramic, glass and tanning/dying: alredy depicted.
; - pitch - included in the concept of timber.
; - wax - included in the concept of honey.
; - copper - included in the concept of metals.
; - lead - included in the concept of silver.
; - pigs and other meat-producing animals - rarely traded between the provinces; cattle is enough to depict such trade.
; - dates - included in the concept of fruits.
; - ivory - compared to the whole trade it was a marginal product.
;
; Info on modifying this file:
; * it seems that between lined "mine" and "type   gold" must only be 2 blank spaces.
; * be aware that simply putting a .cas file from another mod to present a resource on map doesn't work (results in CTD).
; * however, changing the .tga icon to present them in city-panels is straightforward, just replace the file.
; * adding here "has_mine" is not enough for the resource to have mines, you also need to add this condition in EDB
